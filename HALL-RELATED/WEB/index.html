Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum capacity of your venue? +
Our Grand Ballroom can accommodate up to 800 guests in theater-style seating, 500 for banquets, and 400 in classroom setup. For larger events, we can utilize multiple spaces simultaneously.
Do you provide in-house catering? +
Yes, we have an exceptional in-house catering team led by our executive chef. We offer a variety of menu options and can accommodate dietary restrictions and special requests.
Can I bring my own vendors? +
While we have preferred vendors we work with regularly, you're welcome to bring your own vendors for certain services. All external vendors must be approved in advance and carry proper insurance.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Our cancellation policy varies based on the type of event and how far in advance you're canceling. Generally, deposits are non-refundable if canceled within 90 days of the event. Please contact us for specific details related to your event.
Is parking available for guests? +
Yes, we have ample complimentary parking for up to 500 vehicles. We also offer valet service for an additional fee.
